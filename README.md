    <h2>Project Overview</h2>
    <p>
      This project analyses ecommerce and store-level revenue performance for 
      <strong>BrightWave Retailers</strong>, a national retail chain with over 50 stores 
      and a growing online presence.
    </p>
    <p>
      The goal of the analysis is to understand revenue trends, key drivers of performance,
      customer behaviour, and the contribution of stores and product categories in order
      to support <strong>data-driven business decisions</strong>.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="section">
    <h2>Business Objectives</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Understand monthly revenue performance and trends</li>
      <li>Identify key drivers behind revenue increases and decreases</li>
      <li>Analyse customer purchasing behaviour</li>
      <li>Evaluate store-level and product-category contributions</li>
      <li>Assess the balance between new and returning customers</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="section">
    <h2>Tools & Techniques Used</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Microsoft Excel</li>
      <li>PivotTables & PivotCharts</li>

      <li>Customer segmentation (New vs Returning)</li>
      <li>Store-level and product-category performance analysis</li>
      <li>Data visualisation and executive-style reporting</li>
    </ul>
